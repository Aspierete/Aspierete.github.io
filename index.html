Welcome!

Hello, hitchhiker of the internet! Welcome to my website.

This is my personal wiki. I'll post my thoughts, feelings, and knowledge on my interests and experiences.

I plan to mostly write about programming, specifically graphics and video games. However, since this page is about me, you can find anything that I'm interested in —from Rubik's cubes and games to daily feelings, politics, philosophy, etc.

I've wanted to create a blog for the last couple of years, but reasons like lack of confidence, laziness, depression, etc., have kept me from doing it. Finally, I'm here. I am also happy to find an alternative to modern social media. I'm really tired of censorship, hateful comments, echo chambers, and many more negative impacts that social media has brought. This website —like the rest of similar websites— is an alternative to the centralization of the internet. Another purpose of this website is to share pure knowledge from my areas of interest because, nowadays, thanks to generative AI content, finding useful and correct information is almost impossible.

I hope you enjoy exploring my website. Have a great day!
